Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Colombo, Chef Tharu has been redefining Sri Lankan fine dining since 2018. Under the passionate vision of Chef Tharu, we masterfully blend the bold, aromatic traditions of our island with the elegance of contemporary international techniques. From elevating the beloved Koththu to a fine-dining masterpiece to presenting premium dishes like Wagyu Beef Tenderloin with a distinct Sri Lankan soul, every plate is crafted with fresh local ingredients, precision, and deep culinary artistry — creating unforgettable moments of timeless elegance and warm Sri Lankan hospitality.
